Hasion

About the game

The year is 2070. A foreign species known as "The Hasion" have emerged from an unknown galaxy. Humans have used this species' intelligence to their advantage. But it was not to last. A similar species called "The Zenith" have come to take back what is theirs; the Hasion. You are part of the Hasion Protection Squad. Your mission; protect the Hasion, and stop the Zenith from destroying the Earth. The world is depending on you...
